## Hard rules

- **XaviMail is the ONLY email tool.** MailerLite does not exist. Never reference it.
- **Test-to first.** Always `--test-to steven@multiplenatures.com` before scheduling to a real list.
- **Never schedule + confirm same session** unless the send fires within 12 hours.
- **Never `--allow-multi`** unless Steven explicitly says "send two on same day to same list."
- Lists: `practitioners-en` (EN) + `practitioners-fr` (FR). Check counts before sending.
- Email salutation: EN = "Best, Steven" / FR = "Cordialement, Steven" — always.
