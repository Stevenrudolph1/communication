Starting May 1, the MN Practitioner Trainer Program is open.

It's self-paced — you start when you're ready and move at your own speed.

The program is for practitioners who want to train others in MN: running workshops, working with teams or schools, onboarding organizations that need more than one-to-one sessions. It covers what the practitioner course doesn't — facilitation, group dynamics, the specific demands of teaching a framework to people who will then use it with others.

If you've found yourself explaining MN to managers, school staff, or colleagues on the fly and thinking "there should be a more structured way to do this" — this is that.
