# XaviMail — Shriya's Guide

XaviMail is our practitioner mailing system. It replaced MailerLite in April 2026.

---

## What it is

XaviMail manages two practitioner email lists:

| List | Who's on it |
|------|-------------|
| **EN practitioners** | English-speaking practitioners (~12 people) |
| **FR practitioners** | French-speaking practitioners (~104 people) |

When Steven sends a campaign, it goes out through Amazon SES from `steven@multiplenatures.com`. Unsubscribes and bounces are handled automatically.

---

## What you can do — through Telegram

You manage the lists by messaging **Xavi** on Telegram. Just write naturally — she understands plain language.

### See the lists

> "How many practitioners do we have?"
> "Show me the EN list"
> "List the French practitioners"

Xavi will show you the names, emails, and counts.

### Add a new practitioner

> "There's a new practitioner: Jon Smith, jon@example.com, speaks English, from Canada"

Xavi needs four things before she can add someone:
- Email address
- First name + last name
- Language (EN or FR)

Country is optional. If anything is missing, she'll ask.

### Look someone up

> "Is sarah@example.com on our list?"
> "Check if Corinne is subscribed"

Xavi will tell you which list they're on and whether they're active.

### Remove someone

> "Remove jon@example.com — he unsubscribed"
> "Take sarah@example.com off the FR list"

Xavi will ask you to confirm before removing anyone.

### See send history

> "How did the last email do?"
> "Show me stats"

Xavi will show recent campaigns and delivery counts.

---

## What only Steven can do

**Sending emails.** Only Steven triggers campaigns. If you ask Xavi to send an email to the list, she'll tell you it requires Steven.

Everything else — adding, viewing, removing, looking up — is available to you through Telegram.

---

## What happens when someone unsubscribes

They click the unsubscribe link in the email. The system marks them automatically. They won't receive future emails. You don't need to do anything.
